Personal Training

Currently I am training out of Habitual Fitness in Mississauga. My style of training ranges from weight loss, functional training, muscle building (bodybuilding). If you're looking to be leaner and more defined, and learn how to train properly then I'm the girl for you! There are several different personal training packages and offers available if you choose to work with me. Depending on your goals and your needs, I can offer both short term and long term one on one personal training. Please contact me for rates and packages. Prices and packages are not available online because each training package is customized to each individual. If you choose to train with a partner or friend, I do offer partner training and small group training packages as well. Again, please contact me for further information.
